2026 Rivian R1T Quad Motor Is the Quickest Truck We've Tested

By Mike Sutton • Published: Nov 28, 2025

Rivian's updated R1T model is not only intensely quick for a pickup but was also quicker than the 60 mph in only 2.6 seconds and covers the quarter-mile in 11.2 seconds at 117 mph.

While the Tesla Cybertruck Beast matches the Rivian's zero-to-60-mph time, the R1T bested it around our test track at 1:06.5, about three-tenths behind by 100 mph.

The quad-motor electric pickup we've tested, a 2024 Ford F-150 Lightning, needs a comparatively tepid 4.0 seconds to hit 60.
